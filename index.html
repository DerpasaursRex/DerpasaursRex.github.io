Mr. Smiles

My shadow, 2020

This character is one of my favorites and has gone through many re-works in my life. He has gone from another characters split personality, to the other half to my insert character. Smiles is meant to be my chaotic side who likes to envision dark scenarios and mess with people no matter the consequences. He is fun to draw as he is incredibly simple, yet if I do it slightly wrong he looks odd. This version was created for my first website that I hid in the background. I really loved how it turned out and wish to contine developing my characters to make something actually more visually pleasing than just a shadow.
