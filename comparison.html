A Retrospective


Javascript Self-Portrait

A challenge, October 6,2020

We had to code an image Javascript and it was one of the worst experiences I had in this class. Not only was it hard to put the shapes in the right place, but I don't like the result. Even the newer one I made on the right with a simpler design turned out a little odd looking. These didn't look like their designs I made orginally which disappointed me a lot. Since I love the designs of these two characters and they really mean a lot to me. But I needed to do this, I need to experiment with new mediums in order to grow. So, even though I'm not happy with these two pieces, I'm glad I got to work with something outside my comfort zone and maybe I'll try it again one day.
